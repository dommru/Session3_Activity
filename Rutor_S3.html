House Stark

Family Background

  • Family Crest: A grey direwolf on a white field.
  • Motto: Winter is Coming.
  • Description: House Stark hails from Winterfell in the North of Westeros. They are known for their unwavering honor and loyalty.

Eddard "Ned" Stark

The head of House Stark at the beginning of the series, he is known for his strong sense of honor and duty. The honorable head of House Stark, who values honor and duty above all else.

Robb Stark

Ned's eldest son and the "King in the North" during the War of the Five Kings.

Arya Stark

The youngest Stark daughter who becomes a skilled assassin and seeks revenge.

Jon Snow

Although believed to be Ned Stark's illegitimate son, Jon is a central character and plays a crucial role in the series.

The Stark family's direwolf sigil is a nod to their connection with the North and the direwolves found in the wild. When the Stark children find a litter of direwolf pups in the series, it symbolizes their unique destinies and deep connection to the North.

House Baratheon

  • Family Crest: A crowned black stag on a gold field.
  • Motto: Ours is the Fury.
  • Description: House Baratheon is originally from the Stormlands, but they later rise to power in King's Landing through Robert's Rebellion.

Robert Baratheon

The King who rules at the beginning of the series, he led the rebellion against the Targaryens.

Stannis Baratheon

Robert's younger brother and a stern, determined leader who seeks the Iron Throne.

Renly Baratheon

The youngest brother of Robert and Stannis, he also vies for the throne.

Gendry

Although not raised as a Baratheon, he is revealed to be Robert's bastard son, becoming a key character in later seasons.

The Baratheon family's motto, "Ours is the Fury," reflects their aggressive and assertive nature. This motto was adopted after Robert's Rebellion, symbolizing their rise to power through conflict and strength.

House Lannister

  • Family Crest: A golden lion on a crimson field.
  • Motto: A Lannister Always Pays His Debts
  • Description: House Lannister is one of the wealthiest and most powerful houses in Westeros, known for their cunning and ruthlessness.

Tywin Lannister

The head of House Lannister, a shrewd and ruthless strategist.

Tyrion Lannister

Tywin's youngest son, a witty and intelligent man with a fondness for books and wine.

Cersei Lannister

Tywin's only daughter, who desires power and is willing to go to great lengths to attain it.

Jaime Lannister

Tywin's eldest son and a skilled knight, known for his complicated relationship with his sister Cersei, known for his nickname, the "Kingslayer."

The unofficial Lannister motto, "A Lannister Always Pays His Debts," underscores the family's focus on wealth, power, and revenge. It serves as a reminder of their determination to settle scores and maintain their financial strength at any cost.
